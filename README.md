# 🌳 FRA360 – AI-Powered FRA Atlas & Decision Support System

🚀 **FRA360** is an integrated **AI + WebGIS platform** designed to digitize, monitor, and optimize the implementation of the **Forest Rights Act (FRA), 2006**. It leverages **Google AI, Machine Learning, and GIS technologies** to streamline claim processing, improve transparency, and enable intelligent, data-driven governance.

---

## 🌍 Problem Overview

The implementation of the Forest Rights Act faces critical challenges:

- 📄 Manual, paper-based workflows  
- ⏳ Delayed claim processing and approvals  
- ❌ High rejection rates due to insufficient evidence  
- 🔗 Poor coordination across departments  
- 🌐 No centralized, real-time monitoring system  
- 📉 Limited accessibility for tribal communities  

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**FRA360** transforms the FRA ecosystem into a **digital, intelligent, and scalable platform**.

---

## 🔑 Core Features

### 🗺️ Centralized FRA Atlas
- Unified digital repository of all claims and land titles  
- GeoJSON-based mapping of land boundaries  
- Interactive visualization using WebGIS  

### 🔢 Unique Claim Identification System
- 13-digit acknowledgment ID  
- Links claimant, land details, claim status, and scheme eligibility  

### 🔄 Real-Time Claim Tracking
Track the complete lifecycle:


### 🤖 AI-Powered Decision Support System (DSS)
- Intelligent scheme recommendations (e.g., PM-KISAN, MGNREGA)  
- AI-assisted claim validation  
- Reduces rejection due to missing or incorrect data  

### 📊 Role-Based Dashboards
Custom dashboards for:
- Government authorities  
- District committees  
- NGOs  
- Claimants

## 🧠 Google AI Integration

FRA360 integrates Google AI technologies to enhance intelligence and scalability:

### ✨ Gemini (via Vertex AI)
- Processes and understands claim data  
- Supports decision-making and scheme recommendations  

### ☁️ Vertex AI
- Enables scalable deployment of AI models  
- Handles real-time inference for claim processing  

### 🔍 (Planned) Google Vision AI
- Enhances OCR accuracy for document digitization  

---

## ⚙️ System Workflow

1. 📥 User uploads claim documents  
2. 🔍 OCR extracts structured information  
3. 🗺️ Land mapped using GIS  
4. 🤖 AI validates and analyzes claim  
5. 🧑‍💼 Authorities verify and review  
6. ✅ Claim approved/rejected  
7. 🎯 System recommends eligible schemes
